Facilitation sequence (quick)

  1. Set the norm once (quick corrections welcome).
  2. Ask early, reflect back once, then use the name.
  3. Repair quickly and return to the topic.
  4. Offer private correction paths when needed.

What to avoid

Do not guess from appearance, do not ask for origins, and do not turn corrections into long apologies.
